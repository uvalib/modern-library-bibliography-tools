469b

Title page with Fujita torchbearer; 7½ inch format (1969/70)

Title as 469a except line 9: [torchbearer K].

Pagination and collation as 469a.

Contents as 469a except: [iv] COPYRIGHT, 1954, BY RANDOM HOUSE, INC.; [290–297] ML list; [298] blank. (Spring 1967)

Jacket: Enlarged version of 469a with Fujita “ml” symbol added to black band.

Front flap:
Few works of antiquity have had a more constant influence in Western thought than Aristotle’s Rhetoric and Poetics. For the distinguished Oxford translations by W. Rhys Roberts and Ingram Bywater, Professor Friedrich Solmsen has provided an introduction examining these two works in the context of the ideas of Aristotle’s contemporaries, and has made substantial additions to the original notes by the translators.
